🔥 What is TKG Gravy?
Our signature cheese gravy is cooked fresh over coal heat and served separately with roasted Khichiya for dipping.
Not poured on top.
Dip. Scoop. Enjoy.
What Makes TKG Different?
TKG's signature cheese gravy is freshly prepared over real coal heat using vegetables, herbs, spices and milk to create a rich smoky flavour.
Unlike traditional gravies, it is served separately with coal-roasted Khichiya for dipping, allowing you to enjoy every bite your own way.
